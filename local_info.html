Local Play

Our Purpose

We are a Non-Profit organization, that is dedicated to promoting the growth of the sport of Pickleball in San Antonio, Texas and the surrounding areas through the conduct of education, training, social events and other amateur sports competitions.

Instruction

To provide instruction in the sport of Pickleball to persons of all ages and/or individuals with disabilities.

Engagement

To provide opportunities for participants to engage in recreational as well as competitive Pickleball.

Youth

To assist local high school seniors through the awarding of scholarships to pursue post secondary education and to introduce local youth to the developmental benefits of participating in amateur sports.

Promote

To sponsor, host and/or participate in events and activities that promote the sport of Pickleball.
