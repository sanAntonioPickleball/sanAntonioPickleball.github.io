CommuniCare Pickleball Classic

September 20-22, 2024

Fairchild Park 1214 E. Crockett ST San Antonio, TX 78202

The CommuniCare Classic Pickleball Tournament is back and bigger than ever! This yearâ€™s event will be a 3-day tournament for 350+ players from September 20-September 22, 2024 at Fairchild Park.

Join us to Pickle for a Purpose as 100% of the net proceeds from this event produced by the San Antonio Pickleball Association (SAPA) and Valero Alamo Bowl will benefit local scholarships.

CommuniCare Pickleball Classic Sponsorship Opportunities

Platinum Partner: $5,000 investment
  • Name and logo recognition to promote event on the Bowl website, in a Bowl newsletter story, and via the SAPA Facebook page
  • Logo recognition on a solicitation email promoting the event to 60,000 persons in the Valero Alamo Bowl database
  • Logo recognition on the official CommuniCare Classic t-shirt given to every participant
  • Onsite logo recognition at the CommuniCare Classic Pickleball Tournament
  • Opportunity to provide a giveaway item to all participants and tournament guests
  • Opportunity to set up a display table all 3 days of the tournament in a 10x10 space
  • Four entries into the CommuniCare Classic Pickleball Tournament
  • Valero Alamo Bowl promotional package that includes four lower level sideline game tickets with access to the Touchdown Club, and an onsite parking pass as well as your company name on the thank you videowall at the Valero Alamo Bowl
Gold Partner Investment (3 available): $3,000
  • Onsite logo recognition at the CommuniCare Classic Pickleball Tournament
  • Opportunity to set up a display table all 3 days of the tournament in a 10x10 space
  • Opportunity to provide a giveaway item to all participants and tournament guests
  • Two entries into the CommuniCare Classic Pickleball Tournament
Event Vendor (limited availability): $1,000 or Donate In-Kind Products for Silent Auction or Raffles
  • Onsite logo recognition at the CommuniCare Classic Pickleball Tournament
  • Opportunity to set up a display table all 3 days of the tournament in a 10x10 space
